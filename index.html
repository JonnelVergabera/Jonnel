Human Computer Interaction I - BSIT 3-Y1-1


HCI - (Human Computer Interaction) is the study of how people interact with
computers and to what extent computers are or are not developed for successful
interaction with human beings.
Why should we learn Human Computer Interaction?
HCI is a vital factor when developing structures that are not only
functional but also accessible to persons with disabilities. HCI's central
concept is to have secure, accessible and effective systems for all, including
those with a variety of capabilities and a variety of experience and knowledge.
